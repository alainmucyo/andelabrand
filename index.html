Alain MUCYO - Web & mobile developer

In 2018 when I was in secondary schools studying computer electronics, that’s when I started designing and developing websites. After finishing my secondary schools that year, I started building production ready full stack web applications. From that I acquired project and time management skills, as well as the ability to communicate with team members and clients while effectively meeting milestones and deadlines.

As a web and mobile application developer and designer, I collaborated with several other developers and designers. I prefer to keep learning, continue challenging myself, and do interesting things that matter. My abundant energy fuels me in the pursuit of many interests, hobbies, areas of study and artistic endeavors. I’m a fast learner, able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects and roles with relative ease.

I have worked on a multitude of web and print based projects for a range of clients providing Web Design (Figma), Development (PHP, HTML, CSS, SCSS, JS, jQuery, PHP, Laravel, Vue Js, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts, SQL), database management system (MySQL, MongoDB), version controls (Github, Gitlab) and project management (Trello).

I am currently in ATLP (Andela Training Leadership Program) at Andela where I am learning new programming skills, soft skills, team work, professionalism and many more crucial knowledge.
